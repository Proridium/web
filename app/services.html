People, Processes and Products

Proridium provides superior engineering talent to help managers meet their goals. Our rich experience of the nuances of software backed by a deep understanding of technology trends allows us to precisely match qualified individuals to our customers’ needs.

We provide the finest in customized solutions, including:

  • Contract Staffing (Bronze Level), which delivers technical staffing resources from project managers to software engineers, which work under your direction.
  • Team Staffing (Silver Level), where Proridium manages your project involving your employees and contract professionals.
  • Project Staffing (Gold Level), which provides completely outsourced, turnkey project implementation by removing the burden, costs and headaches of managing multiple staffing vendors, hiring and training personnel, and dealing with the myriad of technology issues that occur in projects.

Contract Staffing (Bronze Level)

When it comes to solving complex software problems under heavy performance and time constraints, there is no better partner than Proridium. Proridium’s staffing services are the perfect answer when you wish to maximize control and manage your own project but need experienced people to follow your lead.

Our project manager staffing services optimize time, scope, and budget by bringing in professionals with quantifiable track records who are skilled in software project management that ensures that the goals and objectives of your business are reflected in the technology that gets built.

Team Staffing (Silver Level)

Proridium’s Team Staffing solution provides a new level in partnership with our customers. With Team Staffing, Proridium works directly with your people, our people and other vendors at your site or ours to fulfill your business goals

Staffing is completely scalable to your needs, large or small. Previous engagements have included everything from managing existing projects to developing turnkey systems. This flexible approach keeps you in control of your costs.

Project Staffing (Gold)

With Project Staffing, Proridium takes the responsibility to deliver a turnkey system that meets your business needs. This black box solution relieves customers of the burden and risk of building, training and maintaining complex software teams.
